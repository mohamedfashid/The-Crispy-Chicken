What is Halal?

Halal, in Islamic terms, refers to what is permissible and lawful for Muslims to consume, in accordance with Islamic dietary guidelines. This involves specific rituals and practices during the preparation and sourcing of food items, ensuring that they meet the criteria outlined by Islamic teachings.

Our Halal Commitment

We understand the importance of halal practices and the significance it holds for our Muslim customers. As a result, we are fully committed to adhering to strict halal standards in every aspect of our food preparation and service.

Halal Sourcing

Our halal journey begins with the sourcing of our ingredients. We carefully select suppliers who follow halal practices and source only the finest quality halal-certified chicken and beef. Our team ensures that the entire supply chain remains halal-compliant, from the farm to our kitchen.

Dedicated Halal Preparation Area

To maintain the purity of halal, we have designated a separate preparation area within our kitchen exclusively for halal dishes. This area is kept distinct from any non-halal items and is subject to regular inspections and monitoring to guarantee its compliance.

Halal-Certified Spices and Ingredients

Our unique spices mixture, the heart of our flavorful offerings, is meticulously crafted using halal-certified ingredients. Our dedication to halal extends to every component of our recipes, ensuring an authentic and halal-compliant dining experience.

Hygiene and Halal

Hygiene plays a crucial role in halal practices, and we uphold the highest standards in this regard. Our kitchen staff is extensively trained in halal procedures and maintains strict hygiene protocols throughout the food preparation process.

Halal Certification

To further demonstrate our commitment to halal compliance, The Crispy Chicken seeks halal certification from reputable and recognized halal certification authorities. We are continuously working towards obtaining the necessary certifications to provide our customers with complete confidence in the halal status of our food.
